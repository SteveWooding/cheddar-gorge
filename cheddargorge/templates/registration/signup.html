Data Protection and Usage

  • Your password will be securely stored, using the Argon2 hashing algorithm, with a per user random salt.
  • If you supply an email address, it will ONLY be used is to send you an email to recover your account if you forget your password.

Cookies

  • If you don't login, no cookies are stored on your computer.
  • If you load the sign-up, login or new word form, a cookie called csrftoken is stored on your computer. The purpose of this cookie to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks. It consists of a random string and links the request for the form with the data sent to the server by the form.
  • If you login, a cookie called sessionid will be stored on your computer. The purpose of this cookie is to maintain your logged in state as you navigate around the website.
  • If you login, a cookie from Facebook called fr will be stored on your computer. If you login or are already logged in to Facebook, other cookies from Facebook will be stored. The purpose of these cookies is enable the sharing of a post about the CheddarGorge.fun website on Facebook.
